Colours of the rainbow.

The rainbow has seven colours.These are as a result of reflection on water especially when it rains the light that reflects on the crystal water droplets break into a spectrum of colours.The colours are for example:

  • blue
  • yellow
  • red
  • green
  • orange
  • indigo
  • violet
